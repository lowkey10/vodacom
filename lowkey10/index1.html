What is Gender Based Violence?

The website is going to cover some of the most important key topics about GBV.It's so significant to be conscious of the:causes, the effects and reaction of the whole world towards GBV. It really is unsettling to discover how high the rates of this violence are, considering the fact that it is an issue discussed everywhere by everyone. Furthermore, regardless of how common this subject is, thousands of women and young girls are still being abused and being silenced. As we all know, it is mostly women who are affected by GBV but that doesn't mean men don't suffer this tremendous type of abuse. In case you are one of the people who's looking for answers on this matter, you've come to the right place for we'll be focusing on informing you about what the world considers gender based violence to be since everyone seems to have an opinion of their own. That's onlu the starting point as we wiil further go into details on all the aspects of this topic with not only a people's perspective but a broad angle of view that covers almost everyone's point of view.
